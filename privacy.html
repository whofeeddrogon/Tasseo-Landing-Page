Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tasseo. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

To provide you with the most accurate mystical insights and a personalized experience, we may collect the following information:

  • Identity Data: Name or nickname.
  • Spiritual Data: Birth date, zodiac sign, Moon sign, Rising sign, and MBTI profile (if provided).
  • Imagery: Photos of coffee grounds or tea leaves that you upload for interpretation.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as ritual frequency and preferences.

Note: We do not collect or store your precise birth time or birth location.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • To generate personalized fortune readings and astrological insights.
  • To provide the Oracle chat experience using ritual history stored locally on your device.
  • To track your progress and achievements within the app.
  • To facilitate in-app purchases and subscription rewards.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your privacy is our priority. Your ritual conversations and most profile data are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store your chat history on our servers. If you delete the app, locally stored data is removed permanently unless backed up via system tools.

Photos uploaded for analysis are processed securely. We do not maintain a permanent database of your personal images on our servers beyond the processing period required to interpret the symbols.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to enhance our app's functionality:

  • Firebase: For anonymous analytics and secure data synchronization.
  • RevenueCat: To manage premium subscriptions and restore purchases.
  • Google AdMob: To provide rewarded ads that grant you cosmic credits.

These services may collect device identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. You can clear your "Knowledge Base" (the Oracle's memory) directly from the settings menu within the app.

7. Contact Us
