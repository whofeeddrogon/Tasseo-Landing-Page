Common Inquiries

The Oracle told me something I don't understand.

Mystical insights can be cryptic. Try asking a follow-up question directly in the chat. Your ritual conversation stays on your device, so you can return to it locally and continue the same reading.

I lost my credits. What happened?

Credits are consumed upon ritual performance and deep interactions. Premium seekers receive a weekly ritual pool that refills every Sunday.

Is my data private?

Your readings and ritual conversations are stored locally on your phone. We do not collect or store your chat history on our servers, and we do not share it with outside parties.
